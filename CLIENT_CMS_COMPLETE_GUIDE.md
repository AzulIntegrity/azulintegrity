## üè† Managing Homepage Content

### Changing Your Main Hero Section
1. **Click "Homepage Content"**
2. **Click "Hero Section"**
3. **Edit these fields:**
   - **Main Headline:** Your big title text
   - **Subtitle:** Supporting text below the title
   - **Call-to-Action Button Text:** Change button wording (link stays the same)
4. **Click "Save Draft"** (FREE - no credits used)

### Editing Your SEO Keywords
1. **Click "Homepage Content"**
2. **Click "SEO Keywords"**
3. **Edit "Additional Keywords" list:**
   - You can add up to 5 flexible keywords
   - Core CPA keywords are automatically included
   - Examples: "tax help", "bookkeeping support", "financial advice"
4. **Click "Save Draft"**

### Updating Your Call-to-Action Section
1. **Click "Homepage Content"**
2. **Click "Call-to-Action Section"**
3. **Edit:**
   - **Heading:** Main CTA title
   - **Description:** Supporting text
   - **Button Text:** Change button wording
4. **Click "Save Draft"**

## üéØ Publishing Your Changes (SAVE CREDITS!)

### The Smart Way (Recommended)
1. **Make all your changes** using "Save Draft" on multiple pages
2. **Click the "Workflow" tab** at the top
3. **Review all your draft changes**
4. **Click "Publish All"** - Uses only 1 build credit!
5. **Wait 2-3 minutes** for changes to appear on your website

### What You'll See in Workflow Tab
- **Draft Changes:** Your unpublished edits (FREE)
- **Ready to Publish:** Changes ready to go live
- **Published:** Changes that are already live

## üí° Pro Tips for Success

### Content Writing Tips
- **Keep headlines clear and specific**
- **Use your keywords naturally** (don't stuff them)
- **Write for your clients** - what do they need to know?
- **Update regularly** to keep content fresh

### SEO Keyword Strategy
- **Use location-based keywords:** "CPA Florida", "accounting New Mexico"
- **Include service keywords:** "tax preparation", "bookkeeping services"
- **Think like your clients:** What would they search for?
- **Maximum 5 flexible keywords** per page for best results

### Image Best Practices
- **Professional photos only** for your headshot
- **Keep file sizes reasonable** (under 2MB)
- **Use descriptive filenames** (e.g., "heather-duran-cpa-headshot.jpg")

### Credit Management
- **Draft multiple changes** before publishing
- **You get 300 FREE credits per month**
- **Each publish = 15 credits**
- **20 publishes per month = FREE**
- **Batch your updates** to save credits

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### If Changes Don't Appear
1. **Wait 3-5 minutes** - Netlify needs time to build
2. **Refresh your browser** with Ctrl+F5 (hard refresh)
3. **Check you clicked "Publish"** not just "Save Draft"

### If You Can't Login
1. **Make sure you're using** your AzulIntegrity GitHub account
2. **Try logging out and back in**
3. **Clear your browser cookies** if needed

### If You Need Help
- **Email your developer** for technical issues
- **Keep this guide handy** for reference
- **Take screenshots** if something looks wrong
